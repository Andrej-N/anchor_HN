About Nevera

Tucked under the building's original timber beams , Nevera is our top-floor escape — quiet , intimate , with a small skylight that frames the Adriatic morning light.

A queen bed , a softly lit reading corner , a kitchenette and a beautifully tiled bathroom make it the apartment couples often book for anniversaries.
