About The Heritage Apartment

The Heritage is the largest of our six apartments — two private bedrooms, a separate sitting room, a fully equipped kitchen and a marble bathroom that you'll want to linger in.

It's the home we recommend for families, friends travelling together, or anyone who simply wants room to breathe. Original stone meets soft, hand-finished plasterwork; light pours in from two sides through the day.
